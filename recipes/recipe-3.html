BENTO BOXES

MY NEIGHBOUR TOTORO

Sending off your relatives to school or work with a bento box — a Japanese-style packed lunch of rice, meat, and vegetables — is a common practice in Japan. More than the nourishment they provide, these lunchtime meals show that you care for the person. In "My Neighbour Totoro", for instance, Satsuki often prepares bento boxes for her father and her sister Mei. For your own bento box, you can be as creative as you like, shaping the rice into cute shapes and sizes as well.
